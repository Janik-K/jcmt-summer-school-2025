Schedule (to be finalized)

The school will include four workshop sessions on using the JCMT:

  • Heterodyne observing (David Eden, University of Bath)
  • Continuum observing (Matthew Smith, Cardiff University)
  • Polarimetric observing (Janik Karoly and Kate Pattle, UCL)
  • Writing proposals for the JCMT (Kate Pattle)

There is also ample time in the schedule for science talks from attendees, and for discussion.
